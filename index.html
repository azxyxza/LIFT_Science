LIFT Science

There is a gap in accessible education in developing countries, particularly for science education in marginalized communities. Our club aims to create a platform at UBC to address that gap. Our aspiration is to create a substantial impact on people from the most underserved communities by gathering the collective contributions of everyone who joins us.
